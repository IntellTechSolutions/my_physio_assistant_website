  â“ FAQ

  For Patients

  Q: Is this a replacement for my physiotherapist?  
  A: No. RehabInfo Assistant is an educational tool to support your recovery between  
  appointments. Always follow your physiotherapist's specific advice and attend scheduled  
  follow-ups.

  Q: Is my data private and secure?  
  A: Yes. We do not store personal health information. Queries are educational only and  
  logged anonymously for safety monitoring. Phase 5 will add optional patient accounts  
  with full GDPR compliance.

  Q: What if I have an emergency symptom?  
  A: Our system detects red flag symptoms (e.g., severe pain, numbness, bladder issues)  
  and immediately advises you to call 999 or visit A\&E. Never delay emergency care.

  Q: How do I know the information is accurate?  
  A: All responses cite verified sources (NHS, NICE, BNF, CSP, MHRA) and are clinically  
  reviewed by qualified physiotherapists. Every answer includes source citations.

  Q: Does it cost anything to use?  
  A: That depends on your clinic. Many clinics offer RehabInfo Assistant free to their  
  patients as part of their service. Check with your physiotherapist.

  For Clinics / Physiotherapists

  Q: Is this MHRA compliant?  
  A: Yes. RehabInfo Assistant is designed as an educational tool (not a medical device)  
  with strict safety boundaries: no diagnosis, no prescriptions, mandatory disclaimers,  
  and full audit logging.

  Q: Can I customize the knowledge base?  
  A: Enterprise plans include custom protocol upload (e.g., your clinic's specific exercise  
  programs). Basic/Professional plans use our curated NHS/NICE knowledge base.

  Q: How do you ensure information quality?  
  A: All documents are clinically verified by qualified physiotherapists, version-tracked,  
  and sourced from Tier 1-2 providers (NHS, NICE, BNF, Gov.uk, CSP, MHRA).

  Q: What happens if a patient asks about diagnosis?  
  A: The system detects blocked topics (diagnosis, prescriptions, legal advice) and politely  
  redirects patients to consult their physiotherapist or GP in person.

  Q: Can I see what my patients are asking?  
  A: Yes (Phase 5). The clinician dashboard shows aggregated query trends and individual  
  patient session logs (with patient consent), helping you identify common concerns.

  Q: What if the system gives incorrect information?  
  A: Full audit logging allows rapid identification and correction. Clinical verification  
  ensures accuracy upfront. Any errors reported are fixed within 24-48 hours.
